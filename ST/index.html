How It Works

Manage your waste in three simple, efficient steps.

1. Schedule

Set up a pickup from your verified home address or mark a custom location for bulk waste.

2. Track

Track your worker live on the map. Get instant ETA alerts as they approach your location.

3. Collection & Earn

Verify the pickup via QR scan and earn Eco-Points and Gems for proper segregation quality.
